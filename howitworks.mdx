title: How Veoi3 Work
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Input">
    Users submit text or uploads an image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Processing">
    Veoi3 AI generates a video, automatically incorporating synchronized voiceovers and effects.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customization">
    Users adjust style, voice tone, and effects.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Output">
    Video can be downloaded or shared directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn $VEOI">
    Active users receive $VEOI token rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>
